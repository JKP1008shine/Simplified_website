Tech Ethics

"Excuse me, Tech Ethics! Why?"

Well, recently I was struggling to decide what I should start writing about as my first blog here at Simplified and felt overwhelmed a bit (to be honest). I recently faced a situation where my attention was brought to the fact that in tech we miss the philosophy behind it, the concept or idea behind it, and what the motive behind introducing them was. And how casually we keep using words interchangeably with the correct one, which misinterprets the concept as a whole.

We are digitising ourselves every day by adding one more application to our devices or learning a new skill. The world is equally misusing these big ideas, but knowing the philosophy behind these creations can educate our ethics, and build a different understanding of technology, preventing any types of misunderstanding.Tech Ethics is a platform where I want to discuss and contribute to these topics so that the real moto of these huge creations can be retained and spread among people over the world who are using it.

Stallmen effect: Freedom of users
